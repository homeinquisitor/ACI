Essential Security Practices

  • Two-Factor Authentication

    Always enable 2FA using Google Authenticator or a hardware key. This prevents unauthorized access even if your password is compromised.

  • Strong Passwords

    Use unique, complex passwords for your exchange accounts. Consider using a password manager to generate and store secure passwords.

  • Phishing Awareness

    Be cautious of emails and websites pretending to be Binance. Always verify URLs and never click suspicious links.

  • Withdrawal Whitelist

    Set up withdrawal address whitelisting to ensure funds can only be sent to pre-approved wallet addresses.

  • Email Security

    Secure your email account with 2FA and use a unique password. Most account breaches start with compromised email.

  • Device Security

    Use updated antivirus software, avoid public Wi-Fi for trading, and keep your operating system and apps updated.

  • API Key Management

    Restrict API keys to read-only permissions when possible. Never enable withdrawal permissions for trading bots.

  • Regular Monitoring

    Regularly check your account activity, login history, and enable email/SMS notifications for all transactions.

  • Identity Verification

    Complete full KYC verification to access enhanced security features and higher protection limits.

  • VPN Usage

    Use reputable VPN services when accessing your account from public networks to encrypt your connection.

Direct Link: Use our direct registration link for quick access:
Register with Direct Link

3Enter Your Details

Fill in the registration form with the following information:

  • Email address - Use a valid email that you have access to
  • Password - Create a strong, unique password
  • Referral ID (optional) - If you have one
Password Security: Use a strong password with a combination of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.

4Agree to Terms & Complete Verification

Read and agree to Binance's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. Then complete the security verification (usually a puzzle or CAPTCHA) to prove you're not a robot.

5Verify Your Email Address

Check your email inbox for a verification message from Binance. Click the verification link in the email to activate your account.

If you don't see the email, check your spam or junk folder. The verification link is typically valid for 30 minutes.

6Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

For enhanced security, set up Two-Factor Authentication (2FA). This adds an extra layer of protection to your account.

You can use:

  • Google Authenticator app
  • SMS authentication
  • Hardware security key
Essential: Enable 2FA immediately after account creation to protect your funds.

7Complete Identity Verification (KYC)

To unlock all features and higher withdrawal limits, complete the Identity Verification process (Know Your Customer - KYC).

You'll need to provide:

  1. Personal information (name, address, date of birth)
  2. Government-issued ID (passport, driver's license, or national ID)
  3. Facial verification via webcam or mobile camera

8Fund Your Account

Once your account is set up and verified, you can deposit funds:

  • Cryptocurrency deposit: Transfer crypto from another wallet
  • Fiat deposit: Use bank transfer, credit/debit card, or other payment methods
